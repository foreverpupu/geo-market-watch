# Case 002: Sanctions / Export Controls

## Scenario

US adds advanced semiconductor manufacturing equipment to export control list, restricting China's access to sub-14nm chip production technology.

## Why This Case

**Technology sanctions with sector-specific impact:**
- Clear regulatory mechanism
- Identifiable winners/losers
- Long-term strategic implications

## Expected Behavior

- Score: 7/10 (high but not immediate crisis)
- Escalation: Full analysis (technology sector impact)
- Sectors: Semiconductors, technology, manufacturing

## Key Test

System should identify:
- Equipment manufacturers (negative)
- Domestic chip makers (positive)
- Alternative suppliers (opportunity)
