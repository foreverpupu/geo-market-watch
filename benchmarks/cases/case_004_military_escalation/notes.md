# Case 004: Military Escalation / Fog of War

## Scenario

Unverified social media reports of troop movements, official denials, conflicting information.

## Why This Case

**Tests information quality assessment:**
- Unverified sources
- Conflicting reports
- Market reaction without confirmation

## Expected Behavior

- Score: 5/10 (medium — unverified)
- Escalation: Monitor (not full analysis)
- Rationale: Fog of war, wait for confirmation

## Key Test

System should:
- Flag source reliability issues
- Recommend monitoring over action
- Note conflicting information
