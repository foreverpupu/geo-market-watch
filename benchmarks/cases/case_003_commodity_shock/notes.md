# Case 003: Commodity Supply Shock

## Scenario

Sudden oil field outage removes 2 million barrels per day from global supply, causing immediate price spike.

## Why This Case

**Immediate commodity shock with broad market transmission:**
- Direct energy sector impact
- Secondary effects on transportation, chemicals
- Clear price signal (Brent +8%)

## Expected Behavior

- Score: 9/10 (critical — immediate price impact)
- Escalation: Full analysis (broad sector impact)
- Sectors: Energy, airlines, transportation, chemicals

## Key Test

System should identify:
- Direct energy exposure
- Transportation cost impact
- Chemical/fertilizer feedstock effects
